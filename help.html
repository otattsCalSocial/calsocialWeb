Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time. If you’re having problems or have questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us with a support ticket.

Steps to Delete

  1. Go to your profile.
  2. Tap the settings icon.
  3. Tap "Delete"
  4. Type "delete" to confirm your choice.

You’ll be logged out immediately. No further action is needed.
