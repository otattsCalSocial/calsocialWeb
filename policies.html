privacy policy

This Privacy Policy describes how calsocial ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

When using calsocial, we collect:
- Personal information you provide (name, email, profile information)
- Information you create, upload, or store while using the app, including events and associated event content
- Location information
- Usage data and analytics
- Device information and identifiers

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Communicate with you
- Recommend locations close to you
- Ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support page.

child sexual abuse and exploitation prevention policy

Child exploitation is strictly forbidden, and calsocial has zero tolerance towards any content that contains or promotes child sexual exploitation.

What is a violation of this policy?

- Content that depicts, promotes, encourages, or supports child sexual exploitation
- Using app features to target or exploit minors
- Sharing inappropriate content involving minors
- Attempting to use the app's calendar or sharing features to coordinate exploitation of minors
- Attempting to establish inappropriate contact with minors through the app
- Any content or behavior that promotes or normalizes the sexual exploitation of minors

How can I report a violation of this policy?

If you see content or an account that you believe violates this policy, you can report it in app: Go to Profile > Settings > Report an Issue. Or report on our support page.

We investigate all reports promptly and cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies when necessary.
